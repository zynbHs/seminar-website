Abstract

Darna Community System is a harmonized mobile application implemented to strengthen safety, communication and infrastructure management within gated communities in Bahrain. The project tackles challenges encountered by community residents and staff where outdated methods of reporting, receiving report updates and overseeing daily operations are often delayed, ambiguous or inefficient. Darna optimizes these encounters by permitting residents to submit emergency and maintenance requests with instantaneous tracking, obtain announcements instantly and secure digital entry codes. Developed using React Native and Expo assisting cross-platform support, along with Node.js and MySQL backend to assure reliability and scalability. Darna also integrates AWS SES for protected OTP verification. Darna's overall aim is to enhance community living by supplying a centralized, intuitive platform that solidifies safety, boosts communication and supports effective management across communities and residential compounds.

Conclusion & Future Work

Darna provides a centralized and practical solution for handling common gated community needs: emergency reporting, maintenance requests, and community-wide announcements with transparent tracking. The platform emphasizes reliability, usability, and secure role-based access.

Future Work

  • AR Navigation: Use augmented reality to guide visitors and residents within large communities to easily locate villas, facilities, and service areas.
  • Smart Home Integration: Connect smart home systems to allow residents to control lighting, security, and air conditioning directly from the app.
  • Enhanced AI Assistant (Dana): Improve the assistant with advanced natural language understanding and personalized, context-aware support.
